MILESTONES

A History of Growth

2018: The First Seed

Launched our pilot hub in an abandoned warehouse in Brooklyn, proving the feasibility of high-yield urban farming.

2021: Global Expansion

Certified our 10th international chapter, connecting hubs across Europe and Asia-Pacific through our central data grid.

2024: Policy Influence

Formed strategic partnerships with 24 municipal governments to integrate agricultural space into urban development plans.
