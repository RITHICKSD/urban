OUR MISSION

The Seed of a Global Revolution

UrbanRoots started as a single rooftop project in NYC. We've now blossomed into a worldwide collective, reclaiming thousands of vacant city plots to feed our communities and heal our planet.

OUR STORY

From a Single Patch of Soil

In 2018, our founder looked at an abandoned lot in Brooklyn and saw an opportunity. Not for a new development, but for a new kind of community. We spent the first two years testing regenerative soil techniques in high-density urban environments.

Today, we provide the platform, the technology, and the seeds for thousands of urban pioneers to do the same. We aren't just growing food; we're growing resilience, connectivity, and a sustainable future for the next generation of city dwellers.

CORE VALUES

The Principles that Guide Us

Radical Efficiency

We optimize every drop of water and every watt of solar energy to produce maximum yields in minimum urban footprints.

Social Equity

We ensure that fresh, organic produce reaches every community member, regardless of their economic background or location.

Open Transparency

Our data and blueprints are open-source. We believe that to save the planet, we must share our knowledge freely.

GOVERNANCE
