KNOWLEDGE BASE

Answers for the Curious Grower

Verified information about our global initiative, community hubs, and how to start your first impact garden.

How do I find the nearest garden hub?

Simply create an account on our global dashboard and use the real-time interactive map to locate hubs in your city. Each hub has its own profile with open hours and coordinator details.

Is there any membership cost?

Basic participation in community activities is free of charge. Some hubs offer a "Pioneer Membership" for $5/month which provides access to premium seeds, tool-lending libraries, and advanced workshops.

Can I start a hub on my own rooftop?

Yes! We provide complete "Hub Starter Kits" including smart sensors and vertical irrigation designs. Once your installation meets our safety standards, we can officially certify you as an UrbanRoots chapter.

What is the time commitment?

There is no minimum commitment. Some pioneers contribute 2 hours a week, while others manage entire plots. You can log your volunteer hours directly through the app to earn community credits.
